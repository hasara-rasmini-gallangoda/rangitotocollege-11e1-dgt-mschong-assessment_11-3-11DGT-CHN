MICROBIOLOGY
Cell Theory

cell theory states that:

- All living things are made of cells

- All cells are the smallest units of life

- All cells come from pre-existing cells

The 7 life processes(MRS GREN)

Movement::Living things can move or grow

Reproduction: Living things can reproduce sexually and/or Asexually.

Sensitivity: Living things can sense(and then respond)to their environment.

Growth: Things can grow by elongating cells and by carrying out mitosis to produce new cells.

Respiration: Living things can anaerobically or aerobically break down glucose to release energy.

Excretion: Living things can remove metabolic waste products.

Nutrition: Living things make their own food or feed on other living things for energy.



Prokaryotic vs Eukaryotic
Prokaryotic cells

prokaryotic cells have a much simpler structure

prokaryotic cells have a cell wall

tend to have smaller ribosomes

Eukaryotic cells

Eukaryotic cells have a complex structure

Eukaryotic cells have membrane bound organelles

All living things are Eukaryotic cells

Definitions

Cell: A cell is the smallest unit of life.

microorganism: A microorganism is defined as an organism with a microscope.

Pathogen: A Pathogen is a disease causing microorganism.

Antibiotics: chemicals produced by fungi that kill or inhibit the reproduction and growth of bacteria.

Vaccination: Vaccinations stimulate an active immune response allowing the affected person to produce antibodies without developing the disease.
